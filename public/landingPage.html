What is PUGS?

PUGS is an application for spontaneously finding Pick Up Games of all kinds in your area! PUGS links users together to form teams for indoor and outdoor sports, table-top games, video-games and more.

How it works

PUG events only last for 24 hours.

Users enter their name and email and are given a unique ID to create or join a PUG.

Once a PUG is created, the user is not able to delete their event. However, Users are able to remove themselves from a PUG at any time.
